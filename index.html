The Work

I work in the gap between what the business wants and what actually gets built.

01

Systems Architecture

Evaluating your tech stack and building the technical blueprint that actually supports how your business operates. Salesforce, Boomi, Okta, Jira, Google Workspace, and beyond.

02

Integration Strategy

Building the connective tissue between platforms so your data actually flows. Hiring pipelines, financial reporting, operations. If the systems don't talk to each other, I make them.

03

Rapid Prototyping

Using AI-driven workflows and low-code tools to validate ideas fast. I build working proofs of concept before engineering resources get committed to a full build.

04

Go-Live Delivery

The phase where most projects fail. I manage the high-risk cutover, run the testing, and make sure day one doesn't turn into a six-month fire drill.

How an Engagement Works

Diagnose

A deep dive into what's broken. I identify the manual workarounds, the technical debt, and the bottlenecks your team has been working around.

Blueprint

A concrete technical plan with clear scope, dependencies mapped, and risk called out. Not a slide deck. A buildable specification.

Build

I execute the implementation. Configurations, integrations, data migrations, testing. I'm in the system, not just directing from the sidelines.

Hand-Off

Your team owns it from here. I document the architecture, train the people who'll maintain it, and make sure it doesn't fall apart when I leave.
